INTRODUCTION TO AEROSTRUCTURES DESIGN

Brief Information:

    Aerospace is a vast industry requiring engineers from various engineering branches to build those fantastic flying structures successfully. The domain of Aerostructures is one where Engineers with Mechanical and Civil backgrounds too have the opportunity to develop their career. This program aims to make participants aware of the technological advancements in the Structures domain and current trends and future challenges.

A COURSE ON FATIGUE & DAMAGE TOLERANCE

Brief Information:

    The engineering world is undergoing rapid transformation, and real-world problems are getting more complicated every day. Humans' quest to dig deeper to find the truth of engineering phenomena propels the industry towards innovation and unconventional thinking.

    F&DT is one such domain that deals with assessing the response of materials and structures, in general, to cyclic loading. The domain focuses on improving the design, manufacturing, certification, and operational safety by applying material science, fatigue, and fracture mechanics principles to develop effective material characterization methods, design criteria, and life cycle management plans for critical aircraft, engines, and propeller components. Etc

    This 4 week program has been formulated to deliver and make participants understand and appreciate the domain. The program aims to impart sufficient knowledge and confidence to execute related engineering activities in a typical engineering organization.

Program Takeaways:

INTRODUCTION TO ENGINEERING DESIGN CALCULATIONS

Brief Information:

    Regardless of all the technological advancements in Engineering in the past few decades, the fundamentals of Engineering subjects such as Strength of Materials still form the foundation subject of reference for any machine or product designed by humanity. Therefore, it is crucial for Engineers who wish to work long-term in the Design domain to develop strong fundamentals in core engineering subjects.

    This 7 Day program has been developed to strengthen the understanding of basic concepts such as Moment of Inertia, SFD, BMD and Free Body Diagrams which are essentially the starting point of Design calculations. The topics will be covered as per Global Industry Standards.
    All the sessions are conducted Live with the opportunity for participants to interact with the Mentor.

Duration:

7 Days || 15 Hrs || 2 Hrs/Day

Mode of Delivery:

Online LIVE interactive sessions

Recommended for:

    Engineering Students & Graduates passionate about working in the Aerospace domain. It is also recommended for Working Professionals who wish to gain practical knowledge in Hand Calculations domain.

Program Takeaways:

  • Exposure on classical hand calculation methods followed by reputed global OEMs such as Airbus, Boeing, Bombardier etc.
  • Adaptive mentoring methodology ensures that all participants regardless of their years of experience would find it easy to connect to the discussions held.
  • Know about standard data handbooks such as Bruhn, Michael Niu, Roark’s and ESDU and their applications in modern design lifecycle of products.
  • Live interactive sessions provide several opportunities for participants to get their queries resolved in a timely manner.
